New Client Information

This page explains what to expect before your first counselling session at Peace of Mind. All services are strictly by appointment.

Counselling sessions are conducted in Tamil to ensure comfort and clear communication.

What to Expect

Starting counselling can feel like a big step. Here is the simple 3-step process to help you begin your healing journey.

1

The Initial Call

Reach out via phone to discuss your needs and schedule a time that works for you. We value your privacy from the very first hello.

2

The First Session

A private, 1-hour conversation in our calm Coimbatore clinic. This is a safe space to share your story without judgment.

3

The Path Forward

Together, we will outline a plan for your emotional well-being and decide on the frequency of future visits based on your progress.

Important Notes

  • Appointments only. Walk-ins are not encouraged.
  • There is no Minimum or Maximum session duration.
  • We prioritize your healing over the clock. Consultations are focused on your progress, ensuring you never feel rushed during your session.
  • Phone counselling is available in selected cases by prior agreement.

Safety & Limitations

Peace of Mind provides outpatient counselling for non-violent mental health concerns only.

We do not accept individuals who are currently physically violent or pose an immediate risk to others. Such cases will be referred to emergency or inpatient mental health services.

Next Step

If you feel our services may be suitable for you or your family, please request an appointment. Our team will guide you through the next steps.
