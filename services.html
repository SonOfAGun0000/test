Our Counselling Services

Peace of Mind provides professional psychological counselling for a wide range of emotional and mental health concerns. Services are provided in a safe, respectful and confidential environment.

Specialized Support

Family & Relationship Counselling

Helping families and couples navigate conflicts, improve communication, and rebuild emotional bonds in a safe, neutral environment.

  • Conflict Resolution
  • Parent-Child Relationship
  • Marital Harmony

Child & Adolescent Guidance

Providing gentle support for children and teenagers facing behavioral challenges, academic stress, or emotional confusion.

  • Behavioral Guidance
  • Student Stress Management
  • Confidence Building

Session Details

All sessions are conducted strictly by appointment. There is no Minimum or Maximum session duration. We prioritize your healing over the clock. Consultations are focused on your progress, ensuring you never feel rushed during your session.

Counselling sessions are conducted in Tamil to ensure comfort and clarity of communication.

Phone counselling may be offered in selected situations based on professional assessment and prior agreement.

Important Notice

Peace of Mind does not provide counselling for individuals who are currently physically violent or pose an immediate risk to others. Such cases will be referred to appropriate emergency or inpatient mental health services.
