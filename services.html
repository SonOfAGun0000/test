Our Counselling Services

Peace of Mind provides professional psychological counselling for a wide range of emotional and mental health concerns. Services are provided in a safe, respectful and confidential environment.

Areas of Support

  • Stress management and emotional regulation
  • Anxiety-related concerns
  • Depression and low mood
  • Anger management
  • Child and adolescent counselling
  • Family and relationship counselling
  • Workplace and career-related stress
  • Grief and loss counselling
  • Trauma-informed support (non-emergency)
  • Psychosomatic concerns linked to emotional distress

Session Details

All sessions are conducted strictly by appointment. The minimum session duration is 1 hour. Fees are not charged based on session time. Take your time

Counselling sessions are conducted in Tamil to ensure comfort and clarity of communication.

Phone counselling may be offered in selected situations based on professional assessment and prior agreement.

Important Notice

Peace of Mind does not provide counselling for individuals who are currently physically violent or pose an immediate risk to others. Such cases will be referred to appropriate emergency or inpatient mental health services.
