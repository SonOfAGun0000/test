Terms & Conditions

Effective Date: February 1, 2026

1. Nature of Service

Peace of Mind @ Amirthalayam provides non-medical psychological counselling. We do not prescribe medication or provide psychiatric hospitalization services. Our services are intended to support emotional and mental well-being through dialogue and professional guidance.

2. Appointment Policy

  • Strictly by Appointment: No walk-in consultations are available to protect the privacy of scheduled clients.
  • Duration: Minimum session time is 1 hour. Fees are based on the consultation, not the clock, allowing for a focused and relaxed experience.
  • Cancellations: Clients are requested to provide at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations.

3. Professional Boundaries

The relationship between the counsellor and client is strictly professional. Outside of scheduled sessions, communication is limited to appointment management and brief clinical updates via phone.

4. Limitations of Liability

Counselling is a collaborative process, and outcomes depend on individual participation. Peace of Mind @ Amirthalayam does not guarantee specific life outcomes or cures. The information on this website is for educational purposes and is not a substitute for clinical advice.

5. Emergency Situations

We are not a 24/7 crisis center. In the event of a psychiatric emergency outside of clinic hours, clients are advised to contact local emergency services or visit the nearest hospital emergency department.
